Monitoring coral health, water quality, and marine biodiversity.

This dashboard uses simulated data to represent an integrated view of ocean health across several coastal regions. It illustrates how analytics can help identify stressed reefs, pollution hotspots, and long-term environmental shifts.

Tech stack: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Chart.js (simulated data)

Use cases: supporting marine conservation teams, tracking coral bleaching, and prioritizing cleanup or restoration projects.
